# OpenTelemetry Certified Associate (OTCA) Curriculum

## Exam Description

The **OTCA** certification is designed to validate a candidate’s knowledge of observability fundamentals and the OpenTelemetry project.  
It demonstrates proficiency in telemetry data collection, instrumentation, traces, metrics, logs, and integrating OpenTelemetry with observability backends in cloud native environments.

The certification is intended for developers, SREs, DevOps engineers, and IT professionals who want to validate their skills in observability with OpenTelemetry.  
More information about the exam can be found on the [Linux Foundation Training website](https://training.linuxfoundation.org/certification/opentelemetry-certified-associate-otca/).

## Domains & Weighting

| Domain                        | Weight |
|-------------------------------|--------|
| OpenTelemetry Fundamentals    | 20% |
| Instrumentation & SDKs        | 20% |
| Metrics                       | 20% |
| Traces                        | 20% |
| Logs & Exporters              | 20% |
