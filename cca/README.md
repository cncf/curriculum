# Certified Cilium Associate (CCA) Curriculum

## Exam Description

The **CCA** certification is designed to validate a candidate’s knowledge of Cilium and its role in cloud native networking, observability, and security.  
It demonstrates proficiency in the fundamentals of eBPF-based networking, Cilium architecture and components, Kubernetes networking with Cilium, observability tooling, and enforcing security policies with Cilium.

The certification is intended for Kubernetes administrators, cloud engineers, platform engineers, and other IT professionals who want to validate their skills in modern cloud native networking and security powered by Cilium.  
More information about the exam can be found on the [Linux Foundation Training website](https://training.linuxfoundation.org/certification/certified-cilium-associate-cca/).

## Domains & Weighting

| Domain                          | Weight |
|---------------------------------|--------|
| Cilium Fundamentals             | 20% |
| Cilium Architecture & Components | 20% |
| Kubernetes Networking with Cilium | 20% |
| Observability with Cilium        | 20% |
| Securing Workloads with Cilium   | 20% |
